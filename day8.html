A gift

I want to incorporate the album into this day, and I know I gave it to you when you arrived in Colombia, but the curious thing is that when I think about the album, I think of the anticipation, waiting for the record label to answer me. Collecting all the small details you see on the cover, I realize that I love you in an almost impossible way.

I think of that album and I think about how our anniversary is a way for me to stop, to tell you that this is not a habit, but a necessity of my soul to recognize that every year by your side is the only year that has truly been worth living. Time is what has forged us, the daily calls, the videos and the vast amount of movies we have watched.

I do not love you just for who you are, but for the architecture of life we have built together, stone by stone, day after day. This album is simply the pretext to tell you that, if I had to choose again, I would choose every second of this path with you.

[ HOVER TO FLIP ]

The croweded melbourne night, the anchovies in Manzé and the first time I caught the scent of your Aesop fragrance cover under your blankets, all converge in music. This art is the only territory where time cannot harm us. I think of the moment we listened to those songs, like the first time I heard Happy and Well while you were cooking, me dancing and you chopping onions. I remember the first time I heard Lizzy McAlpine while you explained exactly why that song reminded you of me. It was not just the chords, the piano in When We're Older or the way we found ourselves in When I'm in Your Arms. It was the very air that existed between us when we were together, that air has became our home. Years from now when we listen to these songs together they will not be just music. They will be the scent of the room, the light of the moon walking home and the certainty that in that moment the world was perfect because you were there listening them with me.

If we ever lose our roof, we can always move into that song we heard those days, because time is not allowed to enter there.

2 Whole Pineapples
1 week before

To be honest I was not the only one feeling that electric pulse because we were both caught in the same fever. We spent so many hours weaving plans just to finally see each other and to finally touch and taste the reality of being together. Now that I am looking back from this distance I recognize the raw intensity of that feeling within me. I can still feel the phantom weight of my hands on your skin and the absolute urgency of wanting to devour every inch of you.

It was never about the pineapple itself or eeverything we had planned before. The truth is that I was waiting for the moment that we were in the same room, stare at you until my eyes were exhausted. I wanted to feel the exact moment your breath hitches when I get close enough to touch the line of your back. I wanted to bury my face in the curve of your neck to taste that Aesop fragrance mixed with the salt of your skin, close my eyes and slowly trace your body with my hand and feel the way your body arches under my control.

And finally get the chance to embrace you in my arms, finally feel you and with nothing between our bodies.

Letter for you

Hello, Bizcochito de Azucar,

What can I say about this time together. I look at us and I see our small routines and our comfort and our fights and every little thing that makes us real. You are my home and the source of my desires and my adventure all at the same time. You are the owner of all my thoughts and every small detail of my life. Even when we watched Chernobyl I felt a depth of emotion that I had felt before and I put myself in the boots of the fireman and I thought about the weight of our promises.

We share the same space in our heads and we share the same thoughts even when the distance is vast. You are the most real presence in my life even when I cannot touch you. You are present in how I move and how I see the world and in the things that stay with me forever. I am horny because my body waits for you with a thirst that only your skin can quench but I love you with a soul that already belongs to you completely. You have become the architecture of my days and the only place where I truly want to stay.

Andrés.
