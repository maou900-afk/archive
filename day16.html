THE CONCLUSION

Hola, Hermosa

Did you think I would leave you without a page? I know we are finishing this gift and that there is no longer any rush to finish it, but inside me I don't want it to ever end. I didn't do anything crazy this time, I reutilized the logic of the code on other pages and restructured it.

I know that if we were together, everything would be easier. Even when I make this page, I feel somewhat frustrated. What can I say to connect with you? All these letters and details are made with love, of that I am certain. Sometimes I spend hours thinking about a single sentence, not because I want to be literary or presumptuous, but because I want that sentence to be a bridge strong enough for you to walk across and reach me. The gift is not these pages, in these letters I write to you, it is the fact that my mind has not stopped working for you for a single second since we said goodbye, that I always think of you. And yet at some point I think that these letters should be without words. Just filled with kisses and the great love I have for you. For now, I let the words carry everything that my hands still cannot give you.

Forever yours, Andrés.
