A fight

As the day of my departure approached, I couldn't stop thinking about how magnificent you are the way you look at me as if the world has stopped, your constant need to be near and that gentle, radiant warmth you give off just by existing. I have never seen anyone love the way you do, with the intensity you do. One of one kind. We both knew my time in Melbourne was ending. I knew you tended to stay quiet when you were in pain, but I could still feel it. I didn’t want to leave with the bitter taste of a good relationship gone to waste by me leaving. In my silly logic, , I thought it was better if you hated me that it would be easier for you than mourning the what if. I reached the conclusion that leaving was the only way to remain a beautiful image in your memory rather than becoming a heavy, daily burden in your present.

I have to confess that even today, I still think about how this relationship might be hurting you. There comes a moment when love stops being a sanctuary and starts feeling like an open wound. I don’t want you to see me at my worst because I fear my defeats will only become your sadness. Back then, I thought the right thing was to leave right then and there while there was still light between us, ensuring that light was the only thing you’d keep of my time in your life. I thought that by being the asshole to end the relationship, I was providing the perfect closure. I was a fool, I know that now.

Then you called me, and you were furious, are you a kid? I rembemer you said in flagstaff. You told me you knew exactly what it meant to be in a relationship that would end when I left. That we should take every single day we had together mo matter the ending, living our live at the fullest. I was speechless, your bravery in choosing me over the fear of the future is something I admire every single day. t's your fierce love, there is something moving about your heart, the gem that refuses to hold back. I promised myself to protect it since then, endlessly grateful for your courage to love me so completely and purely.
